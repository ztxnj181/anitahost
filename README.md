## The DMCA Ignored Thing — What Does It Actually Mean?

Let's talk about this plainly. DMCA (Digital Millennium Copyright Act) is a US copyright law. When a hosting company receives a DMCA complaint about a site they host, they're typically pressured to take it down. Most big hosts comply instantly.

AnitaHost's offshore, "bulletproof" infrastructure means they don't act on DMCA notices. For people running commentary sites, content-heavy platforms, or anything that regularly attracts questionable takedown requests — this is a real feature, not marketing fluff.
